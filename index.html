Tips for your visit

I stayed in the beautiful town of Monieux. A medivial town very quiet and just 20-30min from others towns like Sault, Simone la Rotonde, Aurel which offer lots of possibilities to enjoy the lavender fields.

This is a list of thing to plan your visit:

  • Make a daily plan where to go.
  • Bring comfortable clothes as it can get very warm
  • You will need a car to get the most out of the region
  • Don't limit yourself to google maps! There are soooo many fields that want to be discovered
